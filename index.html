About Us

A company built on passion and expertise

Founded in 2019 as a freelance venture driven by passion and technical expertise, Harmony Hardware and POS Solutions Inc. was officially incorporated in 2023. Our journey reflects continuous expansion and innovation in delivering complete POS hardware and software solutions.

Our Founder brings extensive programming knowledge and industry experience, enabling us to diversify into various software platforms that support sales growth and business expansion.

From Software to Hardware, we've evolved from developing system software to providing comprehensive POS solutions, establishing a strong market presence and competitive edge in the Philippines.

Our Journey & Growth

Despite operating in a competitive environment, we've never let challenges hinder our growth. Powered by talented stakeholders, we've continuously sought promising individuals and strengthened our corporate departments.

Our advancement includes sophisticated ERP systems that enhance inventory and supply chain management, ensuring long-term sustainability through automation and systematic processes.

From product lines to manpower, teams, departments, and technologies — every area has been reinforced for future success. This cycle of discovery, innovation, and development reflects our commitment to never stop evolving.

With over 5 years of experience in the industry, Harmony Hardware and POS Solutions Inc. stands as one of the best choices for POS systems in the Philippines.
